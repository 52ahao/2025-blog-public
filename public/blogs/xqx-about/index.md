# About Campus Bliss & Our Team
**Campus Bliss** is a minimalist schedule management tool designed exclusively for university students worldwide. Co-created by the tech teams at **WUAI.TECH** and **VeinSec**, it is engineered to infuse efficiency and convenience into campus life. The product is entrusted to a professional institution for global market operations, ensuring a stable and high-quality user experience.

## 1. Product Positioning & Core Vision
Understanding the modern student's dual demand for efficiency and aesthetics, we position **Campus Bliss** as an **"All-in-One Campus Assistant with High Design and Zero Learning Curve."**

We reject feature bloat and focus solely on the core experience of schedule management. Through intuitive interaction design and robust adaptability, we aim to make checking your timetable a daily moment of "Bliss."

## 2. Key Features
Focused on the academic scenario, we have built a lightweight yet fully functional toolkit with the following core advantages:

### 2.1 Intelligent Timetable Engine
Deeply adapted to university academic logic, it perfectly supports **odd/even week cycles**, **custom class periods**, and **multi-campus schedules**. The card-based UI design allows seamless switching between weekly and daily views, keeping your schedule crystal clear.

### 2.2 Comprehensive Reminder System
Set personalized pre-class reminders that integrate with iOS system notifications, ensuring you never miss an important class or exam.

### 2.3 Aesthetic Home Screen Widgets
Choose from a variety of beautifully designed widgets in small, medium, and large sizes. Preview your day's schedule instantly from the home screen—no need to open the app. They are as much a functional tool as they are a desktop accessory.

### 2.4 Seamless Ecosystem Integration
Exclusively optimized for iPad with split-screen and floating window support. It is also fully compatible with iPhone, Mac, and Apple Vision, enabling one-time entry and real-time synchronization across all your devices to meet diverse learning needs.

### 2.5 Global Language Support
Built-in multi-language packs include Simplified Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, and French, empowering international students and global users to integrate into campus life effortlessly.
